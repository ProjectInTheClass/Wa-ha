Dive Into Waha

IPAD DRAWING APP MADE FOR ROTOSCOPING ANIMATION

Wa-ha is iPad only animation app which helps to do rotoscoping* more fast and easily.
*(Rotoscoping is an animation technique that animators use to trace over motion picture footage, frame by frame, to produce realistic action)
Although you are bad in drawing, you can create stunning animation with this technique.

VIDEO TO IMAGE SEQUENCE

Wa-ha automatically convert your video to image sequence when you import your footage. We produce 3 frame rate options: 12, 15, 30fps which is most commonly use in animation work.

TWO ANIMATION LAYERS

You don’t have to make hundreds of drawing layers. Video layers and drawing layers will be set.

TWO EXPORT OPTIONS

You can export video with drawing, or Drawing only.
